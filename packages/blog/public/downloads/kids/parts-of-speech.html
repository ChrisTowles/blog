The 8 Parts of Speech

Noun
A person, place, thing, or idea
dogschoolhappinessTexas
The dog ran to school.
Verb
An action or state of being
runjumpisthink
She runs fast and jumps high.
Adjective
Describes a noun
bighappythreered
The big, red ball bounced.
Adverb
Describes a verb, adjective, or adverb
quicklyveryalwayswell
He ran quickly and played well.
Pronoun
Takes the place of a noun
hesheitthey
She gave it to them.
Preposition
Shows position or direction
inonunderbetween
The cat hid under the bed.
Conjunction
Connects words or phrases
andbutorbecause
I like cats and dogs, but not bugs.
Interjection
Expresses strong emotion
wow!ouch!yay!oh!
Wow! That was amazing!
